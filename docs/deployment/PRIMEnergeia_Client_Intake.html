Deliverable 1 of 8 · Week 1, Day 1

🔒 Signed NDA

Mutual non-disclosure agreement — nothing moves without this
📄 NDA — Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement
Format: PDF (signed) · Expected size: 2–10 pages · Source: Their legal team

What to expect

Either they send their template or you send yours. Most corporate NDAs are 3–5 pages covering:

  • Definition of confidential information (their plant data, your algorithms)
  • Obligations of both parties (no sharing, no reverse engineering)
  • Term (typically 2–5 years)
  • Exceptions (publicly available info, independently developed)
  • Return/destruction of materials on termination

Validation checklist

✓Check
Both parties named correctly (PRIMEnergeia S.A.S. + their legal entity)
Mutual (covers both directions — their data AND your IP)
Does NOT restrict your ability to work with other plants/ISOs
Allows aggregated/anonymized data use (for system improvement)
Signed by authorized representative with title
Date present, effective immediately
Watch out for: Non-compete clauses, exclusive dealing provisions, or IP assignment language. If the NDA tries to claim your HJB algorithms become their property, push back immediately.

Storage

Save as: clients/[CLIENT_NAME]/legal/NDA_signed_[DATE].pdf

Validation checklist

✓Check
File opens correctly (UTF-8, no encoding issues)
≥ 90 days of data
Timestamp column parseable
MW values are in MW (not kW or W — check magnitude)
Price values present and non-zero for majority of rows
Data quality > 90% (less than 10% null/missing rows)
No obvious flat-line periods (stuck values = sensor failure)
Interval is consistent (5-min, 15-min, or hourly throughout)

How to process it

Run your CSVAdapter backtest:

python -m runtime.control_loop --protocol csv --config adapters/config/[CLIENT].yaml
Your existing data_loader.py auto-detects ERCOT, SEN, and MIBEL column formats.

Common issues

IssueFix
Columns in Spanish (Fecha, Potencia)Auto-detected by data_loader for SEN format
Multiple CSV files (one per day/month)Concatenate: cat *.csv > combined.csv
Excel format (.xlsx)Save-as CSV from Excel, or use pandas: pd.read_excel()
Empty price columnPull prices separately from ISO API
Data in kW instead of MWDivide by 1000 during ingestion
